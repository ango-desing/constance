About us

I’ve always loved things from times gone by, be they music, movies, clothes or beautiful things for the home. They’re called “the classics” for a reason. I always wanted my home to be the perfect blend of new and the classics, and I always marveled when I spoke to people who seemed to achieve that perfect balance.

Story

After having my love for thrifting reinvigorated, and spending copious amounts of time in thrift stores I noticed something that was pretty cool. You could find amazing quality stemware and houseware that was classic, quality and fun. I noticed that the exact same vintage items I was seeing on a regular basis, and now squirreling away in my house, were identical to new items showing up in big name stores for sometimes 8 times the price I paid. These revamped versions were lacking the same quality the classics possessed and were now being donated.

Classic

The final straw came when a friend of mine had registered for her wedding at a big box store for replica amber glass. I offered to find her classic amber glass in lieu of what was on her registry and found gorgeous things that she can cherish and proudly show off in her home. It was then that I saw people love these pieces, but don’t know how to find them, know what to look for, or have the patience to sift and search for them.

Welcome

I decided to bring all of these beautiful pieces to you in one place. A place where you can get great quality pieces for your home for a fraction of the cost, and still achieve the perfectly curated home.

Welcome to Constance where the classics of a bygone era find new life.
