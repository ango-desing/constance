FAQ

What is Constance?

We are a vintage shop specializing in midcentury home furnishings.

Where do you find your products?

We search high and low through various thrift stores and garage sales.

Are all items vintage?

Yes, all items that are sold on Constance Hawaii are vintage. None of the items that are listed on our site are replicas of vintage items or are vintage inspired.

Care for vintage items

Due to these items being vintage, it’s best to treat them with tender loving care. We suggest handwashing stemware and dishes in warm water with mild dish soap. All bakeware is oven safe. We strongly discourage using any items in the microwave. For fabric items such as aprons, towels, and potholders, you may wash these on a gentle cycle with mild detergent and hang dry.

Are all items sold in sets?

Unless the item is specifically listed by itself, yes, all items are sold in sets. We don’t want to break up any families.

What does your rating system mean?

Since all items listed are vintage, there will be signs of wear and tear from being used. We strive to find items that are in the best of condition with minor signs of wear and usage. We have created a scale to let you know just how much wear and tear each item is showing. This scale goes from brand new and never been used to used with some signs of wear. See below for a listing of this scale. This wear and tear could be discoloration or hard water damage with water spots. Each item will specifically list its defect or flaw.


AAA- Never been used, brand new with no signs of wear.


AA- Has been used but still in great shape


A- Used but with some minor wear and tear
